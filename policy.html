Shipping Policy

Delivery Times

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹999

  • Standard Delivery: ₹49

Returns Policy

Return Period

You can return items within 30 days of delivery.

Items must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags attached.

Return Process

  • Initiate return from your order history
  • Pack the item in its original packaging
  • Attach the return label provided
  • Drop off at nearest pickup point or schedule pickup

Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Original shipping charges are non-refundable.
